Server Rules

  • No player kills are allowed unless it is through a previously agreed duel.
  • No griefing of any kind. This will result in a permanent ban on your account and a tempban from Kingdom of Nerds.
  • No raiding or stealing from other players will be permitted unless it is through a successful clan battle.
  • All swearing is to be kept to a minimum. A player will be issued a single warning in game before receiving a 1-day tempban.
  • No hateful, racial, homophobic, or discriminatory language is allowed in the chat. Any player caught using such language will be issued a warning and if further violation occurs, they will receive a 3-day tempban.
  • There is to be no arguments with MC staff in game or out. Issues can be brought up in a separate peaceful discussion.
  • No illegal content such as cracked launchers are permitted.
  • All other rules from the KoN discord server pertain to the Minecraft server as well.

Creating a Clan

  • Must have a centralized base.
  • Must have at least 3 players who all wish to unite under a single clan.
  • Must have a clan name.
  • Must have a clan banner.

Clan Warfare

To declare war upon another clan, you must offer up a declaration of war to the clan you wish to declare war upon. You must however have a valid reason to declare war upon another clan, e.g. they are encroaching upon your clan territory, have attacked one of your clan members, or in a case by case, territorial conquest. Territorial conquest declaration of wars must be requested from staff and will be reviewed to see whether or not it is an acceptable reason of war.

In the case of a battle between two clans, both sides will choose a time to conduct the battle on a certain battlefield. The battlefield will be chosen by an impartial staff member within the contested area between the two clans. Both sides will be allowed the usage of any weapons or equipment they wish, but will not be allowed to leave the battlefield once the battle has begun to restock. Supply trains are allowed, one chest bearing mule per 3 clan members. Once the battle has begun you will be allowed to respawn into the battle three times, though if you die a fourth time you will be removed from the battle and will effectively be “dead”. Which ever side surrenders, retreats, or loses all their players first will be declared the loser, and all clan members that “died” will have to give up their inventories to the victorious side. All battles must be overseen by a staff member who will record the outcome of said battle for the official records.

In the case of a raid or skirmish (no prewarning given) then neither side will give up their inventories even if they are on the losing side. However, if a clan is pushed out of their territory then anything within that clan’s chests can be taken as loot by the victorious clan. In the case of such a raid or skirmish you may respawn back into the battle unlimited times if you so choose to, and will only in effect be limited to the durability of your weapons and armor.
