Server Rules

  1. No player kills are allowed unless it is through a previously agreed duel.
  2. No griefing of any kind. This will result in a permanent ban on your account and a tempban from Kingdom of Nerds.
  3. No raiding or stealing from other players will be permitted unless it is through a successful clan battle.
  4. All swearing is to be kept to a minimum. A player will be issued a single warning in game before receiving a 1-day tempban.
  5. No hateful, racial, homophobic, or discriminatory language is allowed in the chat. Any player caught using such language will be issued a warning and if further violation occurs, they will receive a 3-day tempban.
  6. There is to be no arguments with MC staff in game or out. Issues can be brought up in a separate peaceful discussion.
  7. No illegal content such as cracked launchers are permitted.
  8. All other rules from the KoN discord server pertain to the Minecraft server as well.
