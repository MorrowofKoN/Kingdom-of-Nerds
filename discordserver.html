Kingdom of Nerds: The Server

KoN didn't start off as a movement of weirdos, it started off as just your regular old discord server. In the beginning we weren't very large at all, it was just me and a couple other people that joined the server and winded up staying as well. I spent many a hour advertising the place, doing partnerships, spending time on there, everything that a server owner should do. It wasn't till several months into the creation of the place that I realized that we had something unique going for us here. We had a group of people of all different beliefs, and walks of life coming together as one community and I realized that this was something I wanted to spread to the world. We may not be your typical server or movement, but we hope to bring the love and sense of community we found online to the rest of the world.

We've got a lot going for us on the server, and we hope to keep it growing and to introduce new things to make it greater then ever so that everyone can feel safe and loved there. I'm going to now share everything we currently have on the server, the ideals that we hold dear, and much more!

The Ideals of Kingdom of Nerds

Listed below you can find some of the ideals that I and the other members of KoN hold dear to our hearts.

  • Diversity: Everyone and anyone is welcome to join, and become a member of KoN, as long as you aren't toxic or a jerk.
  • Creativity: We're a creative bunch in KoN, and we love having people share their skills with us!
  • Open-Mindedness: We all try our best to be as open-minded as we can, to be acceptating and loving towards all.
  • Passion/Drive: The members of KoN are all passionate about what they do and your passion is welcome too.
  • Memories: We share experiences and create great memories together as we build our community.

Server Clubs


Book Club

The KoN book club is a wonderful place to get the opportunity to sit down and read together and discuss books. We have biweekly meetings where we all get together in vc and hold a group discussion about the chapters that we read over the time since our previous meeting. We also hold different reading competitions, have yearly reading lists, and hold group reads.

Club Heads: Morrow and Lyn

Debate Club

Debate club is the perfect place to come and build that logic of yours and to open your mind and eyes to the perspectives of your fellow Konians. Maybe you are politically active and have strong opinions on a topic, that’s great! Come and share your opinions and views in a controlled, peaceful environment where your voice can be heard, and maybe you’ll learn a new thing or two.
